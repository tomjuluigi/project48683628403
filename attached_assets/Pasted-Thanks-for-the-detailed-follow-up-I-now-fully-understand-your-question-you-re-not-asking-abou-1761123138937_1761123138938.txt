### üß© Contract Deployment Method Overview:

You can interact with the contract directly using:

* `deploy(...)` ‚Üí for general coins ("content coins")
* `deployCreatorCoin(...)` ‚Üí for official ‚Äúcreator coins‚Äù

These methods are:

* Available publicly via the ABI
* Accept standard parameters (payout recipient, URI, owners, symbol, etc.)
* Require a `poolConfig` (which determines how the coin will be paired, e.g., ETH, ZORA, etc.)

---

### ‚ö†Ô∏è Caveats (from the docs):

1. **Not all pool configurations are available on Base Sepolia.**

   * For example, `ZORA`, `CREATOR_COIN`, and `CREATOR_COIN_OR_ZORA` as currency types **are not supported** on Base Sepolia.
   * This mostly impacts what you can do in the `poolConfig`.

2. **You must provide a valid `poolConfig`.**

   * This is complex to construct manually.
   * Zora recommends using their internal (currently not public) **API endpoints** to generate these configs.

3. **You can use ETH or WETH as the backing currency** ‚Äî this is safe and supported on Base Sepolia.
